I am a second-stage Researcher (comparable to Assistant Research Professor) at the Institute for Systems & Robotics and the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, IST Instituto Superior Técnico, Lisboa. From 2018 to 2019, I was a postdoctoral associate at KTH Royal Institue of Technology. Previously, I held an FCT postdoctoral researcher grant (a highly competitive individual research grant) at IST. I am joining MERL Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories as a Principal Research Scientist in the fall.

I got my Master's and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Faculty of Sciences and Technology, University of Coimbra, Portugal, under the supervision of Prof. Helder Araujo.

My main research topics are 3D computer vision, robot vision, active vision. I am a member of The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE).
