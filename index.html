Short Bio:

I am a Researcher (second stage, "Investigador Auxiliar") and invited Assistant Professor at the Institute for Systems & Robotics and the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, Instituto Superior Técnico, Lisboa (IST). Before joining IST, I was a postdoctoral associate at KTH Royal Institue of Technology from 2018-19 and an FCT postdoctoral researcher at the IST from 2014-18. From 2015-18, I was an Invited Assistant Professor at IST (ECE, and CS&E). I got an M.Sc and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering, from the Faculty of Sciences and Technology, University of Coimbra, Portugal (2008 and 2013 respectively), under the supervision of Prof. Helder Araujo. I am a member of The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE).

My main research topics are: 3D computer vision, robot vision, active vision.
